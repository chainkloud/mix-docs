1. To set up Mix as a new network on Metamask. Click on the network selector at the top of the app and then choose "Custom RPC" from the list:

![](/img/assMRxFOj-hfekokwcTbddggN_-MVohzwcoaqm5IBcGGhf_-MVoj0Lx1BPLqn3p7MB__image.webp)

2\. Then in the "New RPC URL" enter this address: New RPC URL: https://mainnet-rpc.miexs.com

  **Network Details**

  Additional: [https://mainnet-rpc.miexs.com](https://mainnet-rpc.miexs.com)

  ChainId: 761412

  Explorer: [https://miexs.com](https://miexs.com/)

  Symbol: Mix
</Admonition>
